The short answer

Pick Standard

Pick Gold or Platinum if

You have cash flow to cover rent-ready up front and want either standard service (Gold) or maximum included protection with the 21-Day Leasing Guarantee, eviction coverage, and biannual inspections (Platinum).

Pick Plus

Pick Gold Plus or Platinum Plus if

You are an accidental landlord or do not have cash on hand for full rent-ready. Pay 50% up front, the remaining 50% is recovered from move-in funds. Get to market in days instead of weeks.

Pick Custom

Pick Custom if

You are a portfolio owner with multiple properties or a non-standard property type. We will design a fee structure that fits your portfolio with the same operational discipline behind every HBR plan.
